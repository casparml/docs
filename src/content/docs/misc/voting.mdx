## Description

Voting for statalytics on bot listing sites will increase public exposure to the bot and allow you to temporarily unlock perks.

To unlock voter perks, vote for Statalytics on **at least one** of the listed sites.

### Perks

- Unlock all voter theme packs for free for 24 hours.
- Reduce command cooldowns by 50% (1.75s) for 24 hours.
